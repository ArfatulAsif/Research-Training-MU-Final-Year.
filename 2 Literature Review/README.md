### Literature Review Guidelines

* Almost all papers published before **2021** can usually be accessed through **Sci-Hub** by simply pasting the paper's **DOI**.

* For papers published in **2021 and later**, collect **open-access papers only**.

* Open-access papers are freely available to read and download without any subscription or payment. In **Google Scholar**, these papers typically display a **[PDF]** link on the right-hand side of the search results.
